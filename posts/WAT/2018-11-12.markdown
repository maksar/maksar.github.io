## From MongoDB with love

```ruby
# Dirty hack to remove JavaScript objects
output.gsub!(/ISODate\((.+?)\)/, '\1 ')
output.gsub!(/Timestamp\((.+?)\)/, '[\1]')

#Hack to avoid non-json empty sets
```
